My Passions

Bridging Technology and Community: My DevRel Heartbeat

My enthusiasm truly ignites when I'm at the intersection of technology and people. That's where Developer Relations (DevRel) comes in. I believe in empowering developers, making complex tools accessible, and fostering vibrant communities where everyone feels like they belong and can contribute. It's about more than just code; it's about creating an ecosystem of support, learning, and shared growth. Whether it's crafting engaging tutorials, organizing insightful workshops, or sparking conversations, I thrive on facilitating those 'aha!' moments for fellow tech enthusiasts.

I actively seek opportunities to immerse myself in the developer world. This includes:

  • Participating in Hackathons: Pushing creative boundaries, collaborating under pressure, and bringing novel ideas to life.
  • Leading Workshops & Mentorship: Sharing knowledge, guiding aspiring developers, and seeing their confidence grow (like my role as an MLH Production Engineering mentor!).
  • Attending Conferences & Meetups: Staying abreast of the latest trends, connecting with industry leaders, and expanding my network.
  • Contributing to Open Source: Giving back to the community and improving projects that benefit everyone.
